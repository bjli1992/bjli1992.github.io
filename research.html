Research & Publications

2025

PNAS
Manifold fitting reveals metabolomic heterogeneity and disease associations in UK Biobank populations
Bingjie Li*, [co-authors], 2025.
Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (PNAS), 2025.
Applied manifold fitting to the UK Biobank cohort (~210,000 individuals), identifying high-risk subpopulations across three major metabolic functional modules and associating them with 300+ disease risks.
Nature Communications
CellScope: high-performance cell atlas workflow with tree-structured representation
Bingjie Li*, [co-authors], 2025.
Nature Communications, 2025.
A two-stage manifold fitting framework for building tree-structured cell atlases. Outperforms existing methods across 36 benchmark datasets; successfully resolved immune heterogeneity in COVID-19 patients.
Patterns (Cell Press)
Global understanding via local extraction for data clustering and visualization (GULE)
[co-authors], Bingjie Li (co-corresponding), 2025.
Patterns (Cell Press), 2025.
A universal clustering framework using local consistency evaluation and global structure propagation. Achieves 89.6% average accuracy across 21 cross-domain datasets.

PNAS
Single-cell analysis via manifold fitting: A framework for RNA clustering and beyond
[co-author]*, Bingjie Li*, [co-authors], 2024.
Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (PNAS), 2024.
Introduced scAMF, a manifold-fitting-based framework surpassing leading deep learning methods on 25 cross-species datasets. Boosted mouse embryonic stem cell identification accuracy from 57% to 100%.
